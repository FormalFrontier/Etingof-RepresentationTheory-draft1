# References: Virtual representation and its character

## Mathlib Coverage (gap)

Virtual representations (formal differences of representations, elements of the representation ring/Grothendieck group) are not in Mathlib. The representation ring `RepresentationRing` would need to be constructed as a Grothendieck group.

## External Sources (definition gap)

- **[natural_language]** Serre, 'Linear Representations of Finite Groups' — Section 9.1
  Representation ring (Grothendieck group of finite-dimensional representations); virtual characters as integer combinations of irreducible characters
- **[other_formal]** MathComp (Coq) — vcharacter.v
  Virtual characters formalized as Z-linear combinations of irreducible characters
