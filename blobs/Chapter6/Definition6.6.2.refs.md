# References: Definition 6.6.2: Reversed quiver at a vertex

## Mathlib Coverage (gap)

The operation of reversing all edges at a single vertex of a quiver is not in Mathlib. Mathlib has `Quiver.reverse` for reversing all edges but not vertex-local reversal.
