# References: Tensor product of vector spaces

## Mathlib Coverage (exact)

- `TensorProduct`

`TensorProduct R M N` (notation `M ⊗[R] N`) is the tensor product of R-modules M and N.

## External Dependencies

- **Tensor product of vector spaces: construction, universal property, tensor product of linear maps** (undergraduate_prerequisite)
