# References: Representation of a Lie algebra

## Mathlib Coverage (exact)

- `LieModule`

`LieModule R L M` gives a Lie algebra representation (Lie module structure on M).

## External Dependencies

- **Lie algebras: Jacobi identity, bracket operation, examples (gl_n, sl_n, so_n, sp_2n)** (undergraduate_prerequisite)
  Mathlib (exact): `LieAlgebra`, `LieRing`, `LieAlgebra.Orthogonal.so`, `LieAlgebra.SpecialLinear.sl`
  Lie algebras well-covered. `LieRing` provides the bracket, `LieAlgebra R L` is the Lie algebra over a commutative ring. Classical Lie algebras (sl, so, sp) available.
