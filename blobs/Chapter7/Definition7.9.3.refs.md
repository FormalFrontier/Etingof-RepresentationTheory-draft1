# References: Left exact, right exact, and exact functor

## Mathlib Coverage (exact)

- `CategoryTheory.Functor.PreservesFiniteLimits`
- `CategoryTheory.Functor.PreservesFiniteColimits`

Left exact = preserves finite limits (`PreservesFiniteLimits`), right exact = preserves finite colimits (`PreservesFiniteColimits`). Exact = both.
