**⊇ direction** (f^⊗n ∈ diag): Now reduced to:
1. Prove Newton-Girard identity: m·e_m = Σ (-1)^{j+1} e_{m-j} · p_j for commuting elements (not in Mathlib)

Both remaining pieces require the same core missing infrastructure: expansion of products of commuting binomials / Newton-Girard identity for commuting elements in a non-commutative ring. Neither identity is currently in Mathlib.
