## Current frontier

The remaining sorry in `Chapter9/MoritaStructural.lean` (line 147) requires infrastructure that does not exist in Mathlib or this project:

1. **Primitive orthogonal idempotent decomposition**: For a finite-dimensional algebra A, decompose 1 = e₁ + ... + eₙ into primitive orthogonal idempotents. Mathlib has `CompleteOrthogonalIdempotents` but no existence theorem for Artinian rings.

2. **Krull-Schmidt for finite-dimensional modules**: Decompose A as a left A-module into indecomposable summands P₁^n₁ ⊕ ... ⊕ Pₘ^nₘ.

3. **Corner ring ↔ endomorphism ring**: Prove End_A(eA) ≅ eAe as k-algebras for idempotent e. This connects Mathlib's `IsIdempotentElem.Corner` with the project's `CornerRing`.

4. **Progenerator extraction from equivalence**: Given `ModuleCat A ≌ ModuleCat B`, extract a progenerator P of A-mod and show B ≅ End_A(P)^op.

Each component is a substantial piece of infrastructure requiring its own session.
