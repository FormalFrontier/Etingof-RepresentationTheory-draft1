## Current frontier

Three sorries remain in `n_eq_card_simples` and one in `d_eq_finrank`. All require the same missing bridge: connecting `IsSimpleModule (MonoidAlgebra k G)` with categorical `Simple` in `FDRep k G`. Mathlib provides `ModuleCat.simple_iff_isSimpleModule` for `ModuleCat R` but NOT for `FDRep k G` or `Action (FGModuleCat k) G`. The equivalence `FDRep k G ≌ FGModuleCat k[G]` is not formalized in Mathlib.
