## Accomplished

Proved 2 sorry placeholders in `path_walk_construction` (issue #1407), eliminating the connectivity and embedding sorries in `Theorem_Dynkin_classification.lean`.

**Deliverable 1 — Connectivity:** Proved that removing a leaf vertex `v₀` from a connected Dynkin graph preserves connectivity of the reduced graph `adj'`. Approach: built a `SimpleGraph` from the adjacency matrix, proved it connected via list-path induction, computed degree 1 at `v₀`, applied Mathlib's `Connected.induce_compl_singleton_of_degree_eq_one`, then converted the resulting `Walk` back to a list-path via induction on `SimpleGraph.Walk`.

**Deliverable 2 — Equivalence construction:** Built `σ : Fin (k+1) ≃ Fin (k+1)` from the inductive `σ' : Fin k ≃ Fin k` using `Fin.cons v₀ (v₀.succAbove ∘ σ')` + `Equiv.ofBijective`. Proved injectivity, consecutive adjacency (handling the m=0 base case where `f(0)→f(1) = v₀→v₁` and the m=m'+1 inductive case), and the only-adjacency property (all four subcases: i=0/j=0, i=0/j>0, i>0/j=0, i>0/j>0).
