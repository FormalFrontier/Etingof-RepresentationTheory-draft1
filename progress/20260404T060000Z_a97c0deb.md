### Proved `finrank_glWeightSpace_eq_restricted_trace` in Theorem5_22_1 (2→1 sorry) (#2075)

Proved the theorem that the finrank of a GL_N weight space equals the restricted trace of a projection operator. The proof:

1. Constructs projection Φ = β⁻¹ · E_k ∘ I_μ where I_μ is the weight-μ idempotent and E_k is the Young symmetrizer action
2. Shows Φ acts as identity on weight-μ basis elements (via diagonal action computation) and zero on others
3. Computes trace(Φ) = β⁻¹ · Σ_{f∈wt_μ} repr(E_k(B_f))_f = finrank of the weight space
