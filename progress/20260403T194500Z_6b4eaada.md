Decomposed and proved the `finrank_weight_eq_card_sum` sorry in `Theorem5_22_1.lean` via 4 new helper lemmas:

The original `finrank_weight_eq_card_sum` is now proved modulo one sorry: `finrank_glWeightSpace_eq_restricted_trace`.
