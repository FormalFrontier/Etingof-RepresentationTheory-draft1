3. **`charValue_remove_trailing_zero`**: Dropping a trailing zero from a bounded partition preserves `charValue`. Proved by composing `coeff_restrictLastVar`, `restrictLastVar_alternantDet`, `restrictLastVar_psumPart`, and `coeff_prod_X_mul` with an exponent-shift argument showing `shiftedExps (N+1)` restricted to `Fin N` equals `shiftedExps N + ones`.
