## Current frontier

The remaining sorry in `ker_sum_le_one` (line ~585 of Problem6_9_1.lean) requires the structure theorem for nilpotent operators (Jordan chain decomposition). This theorem states that a nilpotent operator on a finite-dimensional space decomposes into cyclic submodules (Jordan blocks). The specific claim needed: if dim(ker T) ≥ 2, the T-module decomposes into ≥ 2 invariant subspaces.

This is a ~100-200 line development not currently in Mathlib.
